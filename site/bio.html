About the author

A life shaped by story and place

With a background in technology and a passion for immersive poetry and storytelling, Brijendra Singh creates novels that explore the landscapes of memory, migration, and resilience. His writing blends lyrical prose with strong narrative momentum, making each book feel both intimate and expansive.

He writes to hold the small, tender moments that shape us, so they can live on page after page. His stories are rooted in place, culture, and the quiet strength of ordinary people navigating extraordinary change.

"I write to hold the small, tender moments that shape us, so they can live on page after page."

— Brijendra Singh
